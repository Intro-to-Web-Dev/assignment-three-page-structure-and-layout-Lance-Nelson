FAQ

Q: What is shoe drop?
A: A shoe drop is when a shoe is released (usually on a website) to the public for purchase.

Q: Why can't I purchase shoes before they sell out?
A: Most of these shoes have alot of hype around them whether people want to resell them or just want them for the sole purpose of wearing them or to collect them.

Q: What is retail price vs resell price?
A: Retail is the price it is listed at by the actual company on their website when the shoe is first sold to the public. Resell price is the price people re-sell the shoes at. people usually resell shoes to try to make profit.
